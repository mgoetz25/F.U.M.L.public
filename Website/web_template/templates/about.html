What we are

We are dynamic team of creative people

F.U.M.L. (F.U.M.L. Uses Machine Learning) proposes a machine-learning-driven solution to accelerate the process of entering forms into a database.

01. Our Mission

Existing fillable PDF conversion tools are sparse, expensive, and hard to use. We want to change this so that users of all kinds can access digital forms easily.

02. Vision

Our software seeks to alleviate reliance on paper forms by enabling access to an easy-to-use solution that is accurate and free.

03. Our Approach

Through a lightweight and free web app, we enable high compatibility and accessibility for users to use our machine-learning based PDF fillable form creation.
